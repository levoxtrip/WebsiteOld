Hallo,
I’m Leon, I work freelance as a creative technologist and software developer in Barcelona.
With a degree in communication design from the University of Applied Sciences and HTW, and apprenticeships as both a media designer and a mechatronic, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to my work.
Over the past three years, I’ve developed 2D and AR outdoor edutainment tours as a Unity software developer.

In addition, I’ve nurtured my love and curiosity as a media artist and live coder under the moniker LXT, exploring the intersection of technology and art. My work embraces an experimental approach, using digital media to push boundaries and create immersive, thought-provoking experiences. Through interactive art and media projects, I explore themes like human-machine interaction and the evolving nature of digital connections, aiming to forge new pathways between the digital world and human experience.

Over the years I also gained teaching skills with giving TouchDesigner introduction courses at my university.

If you are interested in working together, I would love to hear from you.
Feel free to write me at leon.volbers@gmail.com or Instagram.
