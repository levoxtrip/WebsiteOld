bursting glass

"Bursting glass" is an audiovisual live performance based on a recording of a knock on a glass bottle. In the beginning the sound loops and as time goes by it gets altered through layered effects like amps, echo and delay till these create a feedback loop. The bursting of the original sounds through the effects leads to complete distortion and a new rhythmic dimension of the sound till everything falls apart again.

The performance “bursting glass” was part of the one week long short-term project “Sound Design” at the HTW Berlin under the direction of Michael Kugler.
The project was realized in Ableton and Touchdesigner.
