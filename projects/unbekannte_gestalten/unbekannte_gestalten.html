Unbekannte gestalten

Unbekannte gestalten is a networking place for design students, lecturers and all kinds of creatives at HTW Berlin, department of communication design. It is a monthly event where we all connect and meet each other in an experimental and playful way. While working in teams, every team will be given random words to create something new.

The rules:

1. team up: two creatives will be matched together
2. topic: every team gets three random generated terms (the topic, the style and the form)
3. time: 120 minutes to get as creative as possible

All the results will be published on unbekanntegestalten.de
